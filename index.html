About Me

I’m Victoria Dynak, a Computer Science & Economics student at Hunter College who thrives on turning messy, high-dimensional data into models that actually work in the real world. My focus is on Machine Learning and Data Science, with a knack for pairing technical depth (feature engineering, model tuning, and evaluation) with clean, business-driven deliverables—clear metrics, intuitive UX, and dashboards people actually use.

What I’m into: scalable ML pipelines, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), model deployment, and designing front-ends that make complex insights instantly understandable. Fun fact: When I’m not building models, you’ll probably find me hiking national parks, city-hopping to underground house/techno shows, or chasing sunsets in places I’ve never been.
